Mission Statement

Semillas (Seeds) mission is to empower Latino undergraduate students by fostering a strong, supportive community rooted in shared cultural identity in the Latine/Hispanic community. Through intentional mentorship, we provide academic guidance, professional development, and a supportive community, cultivating students into leaders who are confident, connected, and prepared to thrive in all aspects of their collegiate journey and beyond.
